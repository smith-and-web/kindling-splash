Writing Software for Plotters

Your outline shouldn't disappear when you start writing. Kindling is a desktop writing app that keeps your scene beats, character notes, and story structure visible while you draft. Available for Windows, macOS, and Linux.

Where outlines become novels

Stop switching between apps. Kindling brings your planning and drafting into one focused space.

Outline-Aware Drafting

Your scene beats and chapter notes stay visible in a side panel while you write. No more flipping between documents.

References Panel

Quick-reference cards for characters and locations. Names, traits, arcs, and details exactly when you need them.

Flexible Structure

Work the way you think. Drag scenes, nest chapters, and reorganize your story without losing your prose.

Desktop-First

A native app that works offline, respects your privacy, and keeps your manuscripts on your own machine.

Distraction-Free Mode

When you're in the zone, fade everything away. Just you, your words, and the beat you're writing toward.

Import & Export

Import from Plottr, yWriter, or Obsidian Longform. Export to DOCX or Markdown. Your work stays portable.
